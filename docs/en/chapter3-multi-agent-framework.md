# Chapter 3: The Multi-Agent Swarm (MARS)
The **Multi-Agent Reasoning Swarm (MARS)** is the operational arm of the Aegis-Verify protocol. This chapter details the specific capabilities of each agent.

## 👁️ Agent Alpha: Vision Forensics
The Vision Forensics agent is responsible for the physical layer of verification.
*   **Diffusion Detection**: Identifies noise patterns characteristic of Stable Diffusion or Midjourney generators.
*   **Layer Analysis**: Detects digital tampering, such as pixel-inconsistencies in seals or signatures.
*   **Optical Consistency**: Verifies font weight, kerning, and ink bleed simulations.

## 🗺️ Agent Beta: Graph Navigator
The Graph Navigator is the "Librarian" of the SAG.
*   **ROR Integration**: Directly queries the Research Organization Registry.
*   **OpenAlex Mapping**: Verifies the scholarly footprint of the issuer.
*   **Entity Linkage**: Connects parent-subsidiary relationships between global universities.

## ⚖️ Agent Gamma: Logic Auditor
The Logic Auditor is the "Chief Justice" of the audit.
*   **Temporal Paradox Checking**: Ensures the degree date doesn't precede the school's founding.
*   **Program Consistency**: Verifies if the specific degree program exists within the institution's historical accreditation.
*   **Chain-of-Thought (CoT)**: Provides the human-readable explanation for the final audit verdict.
