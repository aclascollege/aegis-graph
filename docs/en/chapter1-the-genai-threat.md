# 📖 Chapter 1: The GenAI Threat Landscape

> [!WARNING]
> **The era of visual-only verification is over.**  
> Traditional OCR and identity verification systems are increasingly vulnerable to high-fidelity synthetic credentials generated by Large Vision Models (LVMs).

### 🔍 The Rise of "Deepfake Credentials"

In 2024-2025, the academic world witnessed a paradigm shift in document forgery. With the advent of multi-modal foundation models, it is now possible to generate:
*   **Indistinguishable Transcripts**: Perfectly matched fonts, paper textures, and institutional seals.
*   **Contextual Fraud**: Forgeries that match real institutional course codes but contain fictitious grades.
*   **Agentic Forgery**: AI agents that can interact with simple verification portals to bypass "Human-in-the-Loop" checks.

### 🛡️ Why Legacy Systems Fail

Traditional Verification (V1.0) relies on:
1.  **OCR (Optical Character Recognition)**: Only reads pixels, not logic.
2.  **Centralized Databases**: Often outdated or missing regional institutional data.
3.  **Visual Inspection**: Human eyes can no longer reliably distinguish between real and AI-generated micro-textures.

**Aegis-Graph** was born to solve this. We don't just look at the document; we verify the **Academic Topology**.
